About us

Service Sluts is a nonprofit organization that matches up veterans with animals from a shelter that could be applied for service animal status. Many veterans deal with crippling effects of Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) from their service, which often lead to feelings of fear and hopelessness. However, it's been proven that veterans who own pets have reduced levels of anxiety and depression. The goal of Service Sluts is to match up veterans with dogs and cats from the Austin Pets Alive shelter and provide them with emotional support animals that will help to lower their levels of psychological stress. The animals on our website have been specially chosen for their loving and kind nature! To get started, create a profile here.
